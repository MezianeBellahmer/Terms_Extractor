1. Introduction
The automatic recognition of emotions NLP term has recently received much attention NLP term for building more intuitive humancomputer interfaces. The most commonly used features NLP term in the literature are related to pitch NLP term , energy and speaking rate, as well as spectral features NLP term such as mel-frequency cepstral coefficients NLP term ( MFCCs NLP term ). Still, gender-specific emotion recognizers NLP term perform better than those with both genders mixed (Lee and Narayanan, 2005). This is demonstrated by (Ververidis and Kotropoulos, 2004) who show that the combined performance of a male and a female emotion recognizer NLP term is better than that of a gender-independent NLP term recognizer NLP term . However, one must know the gender of a speaker NLP term to separate classifications NLP term . (Ververidis and Kotropoulos, 2004) assumed gender information NLP term to be a priori given, but this is only the case in offline NLP term or purely academic systems. This is where we apply our scheme: we precede the emotion recognition NLP term with an automatic gender detection NLP term module that decides whether a male or a female emotion recognizer NLP term is given the respective NLP term utterance NLP term . Since automatic gender detection NLP term cannot be 100 % correct NLP term , this method NLP term could have a negative NLP term effect on the overall classification accuracy NLP term . On the other hand, combining gender and emotion recognition NLP term could also lead to an even higher improvement compared to a gender-independent emotion recognition system NLP term than an emotion recognition system NLP term based on correct gender information NLP term . We achieve classification accuracies NLP term that at least exceed gender-independent NLP term systems.

Starting from the fact that gender-specific emotion recognizers NLP term work more accurately than gender-independent NLP term ones, we extended our basic emotion recognition system NLP term by a preceding stage of gender detection NLP term that determines which gender-specific emotion recognition system NLP term should be used. With two-stage emotion recognition NLP term we could achieve an improvement of the recognition accuracy NLP term , which was even higher for the database NLP term with spontaneous emotions NLP term . For this database NLP term , gender separation does still not solve the problem of finding good discriminative NLP term features NLP term for emotion recognition NLP term , but it leads to a considerable improvement.
