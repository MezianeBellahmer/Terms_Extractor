Abstract

5. Conclusion
In this paper we proposed an evaluation method NLP term that can reduce the cost of human labor on dialogue evaluation NLP term . The main contribution NLP term is decreasing human labor and create a stable automatic evaluation tool NLP term . We created an automatic evaluation model NLP term based on the BERT NLP term (Devlin et al., 2018) pre-trained NLP term language model NLP term and a simple linear classification NLP term that can classify which generated sentence is preferred NLP term . The model NLP term can predict about 70% of the human judgment NLP term with only limited train set NLP term .
Traditionally, text are represented by the meaning of the content words NLP term , from bag-of-words model NLP term to word embedding NLP term . The quality of a sentence NLP term lies on other issues, such as vocabulary NLP term , spelling, grammar usage, writing styles NLP term , and insight on the topic and how to communicate ideas to the readers. It is hard to compare two sentences NLP term with only via content NLP term . Two responses might using the same words, but with some subtle difference, such that one might be moving while another is boring. In this paper, our experimental result NLP term shows that non- content NLP term quality can be modelled to some degree.
