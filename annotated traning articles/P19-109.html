6 Conclusions and Future Work


In this paper, we propose a new task: emotion-cause pair extraction NLP term , which aims to extract potential pairs of emotions NLP term and corresponding causes in text. To deal with this task, we propose a two-step method NLP term , in which we first extract both emotions NLP term and causes respectively by multi-task NLP term learning, then combine them into pairs by applying Cartesian product NLP term , and finally employ a filter to eliminate the false emotion-cause pairs NLP term . In the future work NLP term , we will try to build a one-step model NLP term that directly extract the emotion-cause pairs NLP term in an end-to-end fashion.
