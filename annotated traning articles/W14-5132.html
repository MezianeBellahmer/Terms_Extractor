2 Basis for the Present Study
Speech production characteristics are changed while producing emotional speech NLP term , and the changes are mostly in the excitation component NLP term . The changes are not sustainable for longer periods, and hence are not likely to be present throughout. This is due to an extra effort needed to produce the emotional speech NLP term . The primary effect NLP term is on the source of excitation NLP term due to pressure from the lungs and the vibration of the vocal folds. Moreover, the changes in production can be affected only in some selected voiced sounds. Hence, some neutral NLP term speech segments NLP term are also present in emotional speech NLP term . While most changes from percep-
tion point of view take place at the suprasegmental level NLP term ( pitch NLP term , intensity NLP term and duration NLP term ), it is less likely that significant changes take place at the segmental level (vocal tract resonances). Changes at the suprasegmental level NLP term are mostly learnt features NLP term (acquired over a period of time). It is difficult to find consistent suprasegmental features NLP term which can form a separate group for each emotion NLP term . In this study, changes in the subsegmental features NLP term are examined for discriminating neutral and emotional speech NLP term of a speaker NLP term ( speaker-specific NLP term ) using AANN models NLP term . The features NLP term are referred to as subsegmental features NLP term , since we consider only 1-5 ms around the glottal closure NLP term of the voiced excitation for deriving these features NLP term .
