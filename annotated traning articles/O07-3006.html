Emotional Recognition Using a Compensation Transformation in Speech Signal

Abstract
An effective method NLP term based on GMM is proposed in this paper for speech emotional recognition NLP term ; a compensation transformation NLP term is introduced in the recognition stage NLP term to reduce the influence of variations in speech characteristics NLP term and noise NLP term . The extraction NLP term of emotional features NLP term includes the globe feature NLP term , time series structure feature NLP term , LPCC NLP term , MFCC NLP term and PLP. Five human emotions NLP term ( happiness NLP term , angry NLP term , surprise NLP term , sadness NLP term and neutral NLP term ) are investigated. The result shows that it can increase the recognition ratio more than normal GMM; the method NLP term in this paper is effective and robust.
Key words: Speech Emotional Recognition NLP term (SER), GMM, Emotion Recognition, Compensation Transformation
1. Introduction
One of the natural goals for research on speech signals NLP term is recognizing emotions NLP term of humans [Chen 1987; Oppenheim 1976; Cowie 2001]; it has gained growing amounts of interest over the last 20 years. A study conducted by Shirasawa et al. showed that SER could be made by ICA and attain an 87% average recognition ratio [Shirasawa 1997; Shirasawa 1999] Many studies have been conducted to investigate neural networks for SER. Chang-Hyun Park tried to recognize sequentially inputted data NLP term using DRNN NLP term in 2003[Park et al. 2003], Muhammad, W. B. obtained about 79% recognition rate NLP term using GRNN NLP term [Bhatti et al. 2004]. Aishah Abdul Razak achieved an average recognition rate NLP term of 62.35% using combination MLP [Razak et al. 2005]. Fuzzy rules are also introduced into SER such that an 84% rate has been achieved in recognizing anger NLP term and sadness NLP term [Austermann et al. 2005]. A number of studies in SER have also been done with the development of GMM/ HMM NLP term [Rabiner 1989; Jiang et al. 2004; Lin et al. 2005]. However, in SER, the variations in speech characteristics NLP term , noise NLP term and individual differences always influence the recognition results NLP term . In addition, the methods NLP term above have always handled such problems in the preprocessing NLP term stage and have not been able to eliminate the influence effectively. Therefore, a valid solution has still not been proposed. In this paper a compensation transformation NLP term is introduced into an algorithm NLP term for GMM which operates in the recognition module NLP term . The experiments NLP term with five emotions NLP term ( happiness NLP term , angry NLP term , neutral NLP term , surprise NLP term and sadness NLP term ) show that the method NLP term in this paper is effective in emotional recognition NLP term .

3. Feature Extraction
The emotional features NLP term of speech signals NLP term are always represented as the change of speech rhythm [Shigenaga 1999; Muraka 1998]. The duration NLP term is the continuous time from start to end in each emotional sentence NLP term . It includes the silence NLP term , because these parts contribute to the emotion NLP term . Duration ratio of emotional speech and neutral speech NLP term was used as the characteristic parameters NLP term for recognition. The frequency NLP term of pitch NLP term was obtained by calculating cepstrum NLP term . Then the pitch-track NLP term was gained, and maximum pitch NLP term ( F0max NLP term ), average fundamental frequency NLP term ( F0), average different rate of pitch NLP term ( F0rate NLP term ) of the envelopes of different emotional speech signals NLP term can all be extracted from it. F0rate NLP term mentioned here, refers to the mean absolute NLP term value of the difference between each frame NLP term of speech signal NLP term ’s fundamental frequencies NLP term . The authors used the differences in value of the mean pitch NLP term , the maximum pitch NLP term and the ratio of F0rate NLP term between the emotional and neutral speech NLP term as the characteristic parameters NLP term . In this paper, the average amplitude power NLP term ( A ) and the dynamic range NLP term ( Arange ) are to be taken into account. To avoid the influence of the silent and noisy NLP term parts of the speech, the authors only took the mean absolute NLP term value of the amplitude NLP term into account and all the absolute NLP term values must above a threshold. The difference of average amplitude power NLP term and the dynamic range NLP term between the emotional and neutral speech NLP term was used for parameters NLP term of recognition. Formant is an important parameter NLP term that reflects the characteristics of vocal track. Formant was attained as follows [Zhao et al. 2001]. At first, LPC NLP term method NLP term was applied to calculate 14-order coefficients of linear prediction NLP term . Then, the coefficients were used to estimate the track’s frequency NLP term of the formant NLP term by analyzing the frequency NLP term average ( F1), frequency NLP term -changing rate ( F1rate NLP term ) of the first formant NLP term , the average and the average slope of recursive lines of the first four formants NLP term . The authors use the difference of F1 , the last two parameters NLP term and the ratio of F1rate NLP term between the emotional and neutral speech NLP term as the characters in each frame NLP term .
The structural features of time series NLP term for the emotional sentences NLP term used in this paper is maximum value of the pitch NLP term in each vowel segment NLP term , amplitude power NLP term of the corresponding frame NLP term , maximum value of the amplitude NLP term energy in each vowel segment NLP term , pitch NLP term of the corresponding frame NLP term , duration NLP term of each vowel segment NLP term and mean value and rate of change of the first three formants NLP term . For these parameters NLP term , the ratio between the emotional and neutral speech NLP term was used as the recognition characters.

7. Conclusion and Future Works
In this paper, a method NLP term based on GMM with compensation transformation NLP term is proposed. In speech emotion recognition NLP term , the variations in speech characteristics NLP term and noise NLP term always influence the recognition results NLP term . The common method NLP term to solve this problem is conventional NLP term preprocessing NLP term . As the method NLP term in this paper deals with this problem in the recognition stage NLP term , the likelihood probability of the output NLP term with different models NLP term has been increased or decreased to reduce these influences. According to a simple analysis, this compensation transformation NLP term can reduce this impact effectively, and the examination results also proved it has better emotion NLP term recognition rates NLP term . However, the recognition rate NLP term of happiness NLP term and surprise NLP term is still not ideal, and the test materials are too few to further experiments NLP term . In further research, the authors will extend the experiment NLP term sentences NLP term first, then do some studies, such as adding more types of noise NLP term and the consideration of gender.
