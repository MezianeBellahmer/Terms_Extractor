5 Discussion and Conclusion
The results of the analysis presented in Section 4 show that emotions NLP term are expressed in a variety of ways through different non-verbal channels NLP term . In this paper, we showed that in human-written stories, emotions NLP term are not only expressed as propositions in the form of “A feels affection NLP term towards B” or “A confronts B”.
